Origin

Krav-maga is a fighting method based on learning and development of self-defense skills. It is developed as a military self-defense and fighting system by the israeli army and secret services.
In Hebrew, krav-maga means Close combat.

    A few basics :
  • Run away
  • If the first option is not possible, move towards the opponent in order to block him but also to strike him to neutralise the threat
  • Don't overdo it : the only things that count are simplicity, speed and efficiency
